# Product Definition: Extt - Notetaking for Humans and Agents

## Initial Concept
Extt is envisioned as a fast, simple, and agent-ready notes application designed for managing markdown files. It aims to evolve with upcoming desktop and web applications.

## Target Users
The primary target users for Extt are individuals who engage in writing, encompassing a broad audience including:
*   Developers and Engineers who frequently utilize terminal environments.
*   Writers and Content Creators who manage a significant volume of markdown-formatted content.
*   Researchers and Academics focused on organizing notes and findings efficiently.
*   General computer users seeking a straightforward and effective note-taking solution.

## Core Functionalities and Differentiators
Extt stands out through its comprehensive set of core functionalities:
*   **Agent-Ready Capabilities:** Designed for seamless integration with agents, enabling automated processing and intelligent interaction with notes.
*   **Fast and Simple Terminal Interface:** Provides a highly efficient command-line experience for quick access, creation, and management of notes.
*   **Robust Markdown Management:** Offers powerful features for organizing, searching, and manipulating markdown files, ensuring efficient information retrieval.
*   **Seamless Cross-Platform Synchronization:** Aims to provide a consistent and synchronized experience across its terminal, upcoming desktop, and web applications, allowing users to access their notes from anywhere.

## Design Philosophy
The development of Extt is primarily guided by a philosophy of **Minimalism and Efficiency**, focusing on delivering core note-taking features with speed and a lightweight footprint across all platforms. Complementary to this, **Extensibility and Integration** are also key, prioritizing robust APIs for agents and other tools. Furthermore, **Cross-Platform Consistency** is an important consideration to ensure a uniform user experience across terminal, desktop, and web environments.

## Long-Term Vision
The long-term vision for Extt includes expanding its reach to **mobile platforms (iOS/Android)**, further enhancing accessibility and convenience for users on the go.

## Success Metrics
The success of Extt will primarily be measured by:
*   **User Engagement and Retention:** Monitoring active users and the frequency of application usage.
*   **Positive User Feedback and Reviews:** Gauging user satisfaction and collecting valuable insights for continuous improvement.
